Who We Are

Good People, Great Noodles

Welcome to Zzamppong Zizon. Nestled in the heart of Duluth, our story begins with the Suh family's passion for crafting authentic Korean jjamppong, a soul-warming noodle dish. Passed down through generations, our recipe is a cherished family secret that we take pride in sharing with our community. At Zzamppong Zizon, every bowl is a labor of love, inviting you to experience the rich tapestry of our Korean culinary heritage.
